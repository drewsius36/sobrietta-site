SOBRIETTA

When prophecy fails, survival becomes rebellion.

About the Series

Set on a dying alien planet that bridges ancient prophecy and interstellar tech, Sobrietta follows Kael, a quiet technician plagued by visions of apocalypse, and Agent 55, an ex-operative who’s seen this end before. Together, they challenge the system built to erase them.

Inspired by Halo, Scavengers Reign, and Attack on Titan.

Main Characters

Kael Dravyn

The reluctant survivor with ancient DNA — caught between destiny and destruction.

Agent 55

An ex-Chosen operative with no faith in prophecy — only in survival.
